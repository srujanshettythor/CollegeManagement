      Welcome to Sri Mahaveera College

      Welcome to
      Sri Mahaveera College

      Sri Mahaveera College was founded by Academy of General Education, Manipal and Sri Mahaveera College Trust 1965, with the primary objective of providing University education in a rural atmosphere for deserving students, especially those belonging to the low Community. Although this college is meant primarily for rural area students, it admits other students irrespective of caste and creed.

      About Sri Mahaveera College

      The college aims at training young men and women of quality to be leaders in all walks of life, whom we hope will play a vital role in bringing about the desired changes for the betterment of the people of our country, more particularly of the Dalits and other poorer and marginalised sections of society.

      Prof. S. Shivaji Joish

      Former Principal, D. Banumaiah’s College, Mysuru.
      I am immensely proud that I am an alumnus of the first batch of Sri Mahavira College, Mudubidire. I have been privileged to be a witness to the Silver Jubilee and Golden Jubilee celebrations of the institution. Behind whatever minuscule achievements I have had in my life and literature are the imperceptible favour of my venerable preceptors and benign alma mater. Most reverentially I bow and salute you my Alma Mater.

      Member of Legislative Assembly (M.L.A)
      Mangaluru constituency (204)

      Dr. Boja Poojary

      Year of Study at SMC: 1986-1988 (PUC-Science, PCMS), 1988-1991(BSc, PCM). College plays a very important role in building up the career of a person in many ways. Our tomorrow depends essentially on what we do today, whether winning fame or future, power or position, wealth or goods. Success in one’s life depends on proper planning and execution of the plan. Fortunately, I got through in Sri Mahaveera College, Moodbidri. I started my college journey as a PUC Science(PCMS) student followed by B.Sc.(PCM) in SMC. Five years of my educational journey at SMC has played a vital role in building my career. It helped me to discover my own strengths and weaknesses. I developed self-motivation and discipline along with study techniques which I could use for the rest of my life. The education and experience I acquired in SMC have moulded me to shape my career and helped me to discover the professionalism within me. I feel very blessed and proud to state that I had motivating, supporting, enthusiastic and affectionate teachers. They have taught me to carry myself through any uphills and challenges of life. I am very happy to state that the guidance and quality of education from Sri Mahaveera College brought me to this level.

      Professor, Department of PG Studies & Research in Chemistry, Mangalore University, Mangalagangothri-574199.

      Anand Shetty

      Sri Mahaveera college is one of the top college in Karnataka, centrally located in Moodbidri. College is accessible by very good roads in both sides. The campus has a vibrant and pleasant atmosphere with very good clean and hygienic hostel accommodation for outstation students. Apart from general education, the college is promoting sports, NCC, and all sorts of games such as cricket, volleyball, badminton football etc. college has a well-equipped laboratory for chemistry and physics students. College is continuously promoting sports as well as arts, and cultural activities such as dance, drama, Yakshagana, etc Mahaveera college has a highly learned teaching faculty along with well-behaved, student-friendly non teaching staff. College is blessed with an attached large playground also students are encouraged for innovation and development of new ideas in mechanical, electrical and space equipment. The college management has a proactive approach and adopts an open and transparent policy with students and maintains a cordial relationship. I am proud to admit that I am an ex-student of Sri Mahaveera college.

      The chairman & Managing Director of Organic Industries Pvt Ltd
